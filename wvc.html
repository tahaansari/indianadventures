SCHOOLS AND COLLEGES WVC

Keeping in mind, the growing interest of the youth for wildlife and nature related activities, Indian Adventures offers Wild Venture Camps , an excellent avenue in outdoor education for nature club Members/ School, College students. Wild Ventures Camps, which are specially designed, offers the opportunity to experience the best of Indian Wildlife & Adventure. A fun & adventure filled (04 nights / 05 days) camp, or duration of your Choice, training them in the art of vacationing - the eco-friendly way. Our Naturalists at the Resorts are a wealth of knowledge in the field of Nature & Wildlife.

The Wild Venture Camps are conducted at our Resorts amidst rugged natural surroundings on the periphery of four of the major National Parks & Wildlife Sanctuaries in the country. Wild Venture Camps are designed as prime avenues in Outdoor Education, Exposure and Vacation for students and members of Nature Clubs and Conservation Institutions.

We have designed these special programmes for students where they will gain discipline, self-confidence, teamwork, taking up challenges and above all having fun in whatever the activity or simply plain relaxation while enjoying nature at its best.

Some of the activities we offer during the camp are:

  • Tent pitching
  • Map reading
  • Knots
  • First aid
  • Lectures on mountaineering
  • Rock climbing
  • Rappelling
  • Rifle shooting
  • River crossing
  • Jummaring
  • Monkey Crawl / Tarzan Swing
  • Commando bridge / vertical ladder
  • Obstacle race
  • Trekking
  • Nature walk / do’s and don’t in the forest

Instructors and Naturalists who conduct our camp are highly trained to adhere to high level of safety and precaution.
