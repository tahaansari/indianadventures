Wild Chalet Resort is situated at the buffer zone in Kanha National Park, in the state of Madhya Pradesh , Central India. The resort is gorgeously located on the confluence of the Banjar and the Ghangar river with beautiful and cosy cottages offering a panoramic view of the rivers.

The Resort consists of 20 Single and twin cottages with private sit outs and hammocks, all having a captivating view of the River. The Centrally located Dining and recreational area is the hub of all activities during your stay at our Kanha hotel. Bird calls and the sight of numerous birds flitting around engross you while you tickle your taste buds with the delicacies laid out by the Chef's.

The extended open deck offers opportunities of observing wildlife and bird life along the river.
