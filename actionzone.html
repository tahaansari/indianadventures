ACTION ZONE

Action Zone, which has been set up to develop and conduct most major activities of Adventure Tourism. All activities are conducted under the guidance of our well trained ‘Naturalists’ and our ‘Action Zone’ scouts, and safety given utmost importance.

A list of the many thrilling adventure activities the ‘ACTION ZONE’ conducts are:.

  • Nature treks
  • White Water Rafting
  • Kayaking, Canoeing, Coracle rides
  • Mountain Biking
  • Rappelling
  • Nature Treks
  • Water Zorbing
  • Archery
  • Rifle shooting
  • Camping
  • Bird watching And much more

We believe that Wildlife at our destinations is not alone the U.S.P of the Resorts. We strive to be better each day and also believe in the ongoing process of development, and therefore we come up with new activities each season for the enjoyment for our guests.
