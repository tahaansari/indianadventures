Welcome to Indian Adventures - Wildlife and Adventure Resorts. We offer you luxurious Hotels and Tiger Resorts at various Indian Jungles like Wild Chalet Resort in Kanha National Park at Madhya Pradesh, Tiger Moon Resort in Ranthambore National Park at Rajasthan, Tiger Trails Resort in Bandhavgarh National Park at Madhya Pradesh, Bison River Resort in Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary at Karnataka. We specialize in offering safari lodges in the heart on Indian Jungles. Our hotels and resorts are at convenient locations and are available for reservation at reasonable rates. Indian Adventures - Wildlife & Adventure Resorts are the pioneers in wildlife tourism, owning and operating the largest chain of wildlife resorts in India. The Managing Director - Mr Abdul Kader, visualized Indian Adventures in 1983, when wildlife tourism in India was still untapped.

India for years now, has been known for its Captivating beauty, Historical & Cultural Heritage, and is a sought after destination globally. However, what is less known about India is its abundance of Flora and Fauna, and its variety of Adventure & Off-beat destinations.

With his interest in Wildlife and Outdoors, along with his years of experience in the field; after due research and ground realities, and exploring various exotic wildlife destinations of India, Indian Adventures was established with its first Resort - Wild Chalet Resort, Kanha National Park. Through the glorious years of this organization, the company presently runs 4 operational Resorts, one jungle camp, and upcoming projects.
